- **33% of family offices** actively investing in crypto (2024 survey)

- Preferred vehicles: Direct holdings, ETFs, VC funds, specialized crypto funds

| **Ruffer Investment** | UK | Traded crypto profitably 2020-21, cautious re-entry | Strategic timing | Institutional channels |

### Family Office Characteristics by Region

**Asia-Pacific (28% invested):**
- Most aggressive crypto allocations (up to 5%)
- Tech entrepreneur-founded offices
- Hong Kong, Singapore hubs
- Direct token + fund investments

**North America (16% invested):**
- Bifurcated: tech billionaires vs traditional wealth
- ETF adoption accelerating
- Regulatory clarity improving sentiment

**Europe (14% invested):**
- Most conservative
- MiCA regulation providing clarity
- Wealth preservation focus limits crypto exposure

### How to Approach Family Offices
1. **Multi-family office platforms**: XBTO, Xerof, BNY Mellon alternatives
2. **Crypto-native MFOs**: Galaxy Digital's family office services
3. **Traditional wealth advisors**: Goldman Sachs PWM, UBS
4. **Direct intros**: Via portfolio company founders
5. **Conferences**: Family Office Association events, SALT Conference

## 4. LP Networks & Party Round Platforms

### Echo.xyz (Priority Platform)

**Overview:**
- Crypto-native angel investing platform
- 85+ private investing groups led by top VCs/angels
- On-chain transactions via USDC on Base
- Invite-only beta (acquired by Coinbase 2025 for $375M)

**How it works:**
1. Lead investors create groups
2. Members follow deals on same terms
3. SPV structure consolidates investors
4. Fully on-chain execution

**Key Stats:**
- First deal: $300K for Ethena (April 2024)
- Access to VC-quality deal flow
- Min investment varies by deal

**How to use for Cere:**
- Get introduced to group leads
- Present to relevant groups (infrastructure-focused)
- Leverage for party round alongside lead investor

### Republic

**Overview:**
- Crowdfunding platform for startups, real estate, crypto
- Reg CF (non-accredited) + Reg D (accredited) offerings
- Republic Crypto arm for Web3 specifically

**Key Features:**
- $700M raised for crypto-focused funds (2022)
- 100+ portfolio companies
- Advisory services for token design

**For Cere:**
- Potential community round platform
- Token launch advisory
- Access to retail + accredited investors

**Contact:** republic.com, republiccrypto.com
