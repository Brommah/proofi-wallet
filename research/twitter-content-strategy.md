## 8. Crisis Management

### FUD Response Protocol

1. **Assess** - Is it legitimate concern or troll?
2. **Respond quickly** - Within 1 hour if possible
3. **Facts first** - Data, links, evidence
4. **Professional tone** - Never emotional
5. **Acknowledge valid concerns** - Transparency builds trust
6. **Follow up** - Update as situation develops

### Pre-written Templates

**Security concern:**
> "Thanks for flagging. Our security team is investigating. We'll update within [X hours]. In the meantime, here's what we know: [facts]"

**Token FUD:**
> "We focus on building, not price speculation. Here's what we shipped this month: [link]. Our roadmap: [link]"

**Competitor attack:**
> "We prefer to let our technology speak for itself. Here's how Cere handles [specific issue]: [explanation]"
