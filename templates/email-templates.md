### 1. Cold Outreach - Investor

**Subject:** `[COMPANY_NAME]: [ONE_LINE_HOOK]`

**Body:**
```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

[PERSONALIZED_OPENER_ABOUT_THEIR_PORTFOLIO_OR_RECENT_ACTIVITY].

I'm [YOUR_NAME], founder of [COMPANY_NAME]. We're [ONE_SENTENCE_DESCRIPTION].

In [TIMEFRAME], we've [KEY_TRACTION_METRIC]. We're raising [ROUND_SIZE] to [PRIMARY_USE_OF_FUNDS].

Would you have 20 minutes [SPECIFIC_DATE_RANGE] to explore if there's a fit?

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]
[LINKEDIN_URL]
```

**When to use:** First contact with a potential investor who hasn't heard of you.

**Tips:**
- Research their portfolio ‚Äî reference a relevant investment they made
- One traction metric beats five vanity metrics
- Keep under 150 words
- Subject line should intrigue, not explain everything

**When to use:** Prospecting into enterprise accounts without a warm intro.

- Reference a customer they'd recognize or respect
- Ask for 15 minutes, not 30 ‚Äî lower commitment = higher conversion

### 5. Follow-up After No Response

**Subject:** `Re: [ORIGINAL_SUBJECT]` (same thread) or `Following up`

**Body:**
```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

Wanted to bump this to the top of your inbox. I know things get busy.

[ONE_NEW_PIECE_OF_INFO_OR_VALUE_ADD ‚Äî optional].

Still interested in [BRIEF_RESTATE_OF_ASK]?

If the timing isn't right, no worries ‚Äî just let me know.

[YOUR_NAME]
```

**When to use:** 5-7 days after your initial email got no response.

**Tips:**
- Wait at least 5 days before following up
- Add new value if you can (news, insight, update)
- Keep it SHORT ‚Äî they already have the context
- Give them permission to say no (paradoxically increases responses)
- Maximum 2-3 follow-ups, then move on

**Body:**
```

- [OPTION_2]
- [OPTION_3]
```

**When to use:** Requesting a meeting with colleagues, especially cross-functional.

- Propose specific times (reduces back-and-forth)
- Keep attendee list minimal ‚Äî every extra person = exponentially harder to schedule
- Consider: does this need to be a meeting, or could it be async?

## üìö General Tips

1. **Subject lines matter** ‚Äî 47% of emails are opened based on subject line alone
2. **Front-load the ask** ‚Äî busy people skim; put the important stuff first
3. **One email, one ask** ‚Äî multiple asks = none get done
4. **Shorter is better** ‚Äî aim for under 150 words for cold emails
5. **Proofread** ‚Äî typos kill credibility, especially in cold outreach
6. **Time it right** ‚Äî Tuesday-Thursday 9-11am typically gets best response rates
7. **Follow up** ‚Äî most deals happen after the 5th touch, but know when to stop
8. **Track what works** ‚Äî A/B test subject lines, CTAs, and timing
