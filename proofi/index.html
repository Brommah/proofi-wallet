How It Works

Simple. Secure. Seedless.

No complicated seed phrases. No technical barriers. Just email, PIN, and unbreakable encryption.

01

Email + PIN Auth

Sign up with your email and choose a secure PIN. No 24-word seed phrases to lose. Your PIN is never stored ‚Äî it's used to derive your keys locally.

üîê
02

Local Key Derivation

Using PBKDF2, your email + PIN generates cryptographic keys entirely in your browser. Your keys never touch our servers. Ever.
