Installation Guide

1

Download & unzip

Download the .zip file above and extract it to a folder on your computer.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

Navigate to chrome://extensions and enable "Developer mode" in the top right.

3

Load the extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the folder you extracted. The Proofi icon appears in your toolbar.

4

Create your wallet

Click the Proofi icon, enter a seed phrase or generate one, set a PIN. You're ready to sign.
