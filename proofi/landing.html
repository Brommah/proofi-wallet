// The Problem
Credentials are broken.

Your professional identity lives on platforms you don't control, verified by processes that don't work, trusted by systems that can't tell the difference between real and fake.

Self-reported and unverified

Anyone can claim any title, any degree, any company on LinkedIn. 121 million fake accounts removed per year — and those are just the ones they catch.

Platform-controlled identity

Your professional reputation lives on servers you don't own, governed by people you didn't choose, monetized without your consent. Delete your account and it's gone.

Verification that doesn't scale

Background checks take weeks, cost hundreds, and still rely on phone calls and emails. Reference checks are theater. Nobody actually verifies anything.

// How It Works
Issue. Verify. Own.

Three steps to credentials that are cryptographically verified, permanently stored, and entirely under your control.

Step 01

Issue

Institutions — universities, employers, certification bodies — issue credentials directly to your wallet. Cryptographically signed, timestamped, and stored on Cere's decentralized network.

Step 02

Verify

Anyone can verify a credential with one click. No account needed, no API keys, no subscription. The cryptographic proof speaks for itself — it's either valid or it's not.

Step 03

Own

Your credentials live in your wallet, not on someone else's platform. Share them when you want, revoke access when you don't. Your professional identity, your rules.

// Why Blockchain
Not hype. Infrastructure.

We're not interested in tokens or speculation. We use blockchain because it solves three problems that no centralized system can.

Permanent

Your credentials exist on a decentralized network with no single point of failure. No company can shut down and take your records with them. No server crash erases your history. Once issued, it's there.

Tamper-proof

Every credential is cryptographically signed by its issuer. Change a single character and the signature breaks. There's no way to fake, alter, or backdate a verified credential. Math doesn't lie.

You own it

Your credentials live in your wallet — not on LinkedIn's servers, not in a university's database. You decide who sees what, when, and for how long. Revoke access anytime.

Built on Cere Network's Decentralized Data Cloud — production infrastructure with 63 nodes, 99.9% uptime, and cryptographic tamper-proofing at every layer. Not a whitepaper. Running in production.
